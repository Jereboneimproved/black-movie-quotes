🎬 The Culture Quote Quiz
"Ancestor Approved or 'Bye, Felicia'?"

Welcome to the ultimate test of Black Cinema knowledge. Powered by Gemini 3 Flash, this app challenges your memory of the most iconic lines in film history—from the 1980s classics to the future hits of 2026.

✨ Features
20-Second Pressure Timer: The clock shifts to RED when you have 5 seconds left. No time to Google!

AI Clue Master: Stuck? Ask Gemini for a cryptic, "cookout-style" hint.

Halfway Roast: At the 5-question mark, the AI will either celebrate your genius or pull your "Cousin Card."

The Hall of Fame: Save your score to a session-based leaderboard and see how you rank.

Group Chat Ready: One-click copy-paste to share your grade and brag to the friends.
